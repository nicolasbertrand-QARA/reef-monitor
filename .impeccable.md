## Design Context

### Users
Solo reef keeper monitoring a 30L nano tank. Uses the app at the tank with wet hands — needs fast, glanceable data entry and status checks. Context is meditative: testing water is a ritual, not a chore.

### Brand Personality
Calm, attentive, natural. Like the surface of still water — confidence that everything is under control.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Warm, light, serene. Think Japanese aquascaping aesthetics — wabi-sabi restraint, intentional simplicity.
- **Theme**: Light mode. Warm sand and driftwood tones with muted teal accents.
- **Anti-references**: No cheesy aquarium store vibes (bright blue everything, fish clipart, tropical clichés). No gaming/neon/sci-fi (glowing borders, dark-mode-with-cyan, HUD aesthetics).
- **References**: High-end weather apps — beautiful data presentation, clean and expressive.

### Design Principles
1. **Data is the decoration** — numbers and trends ARE the visual interest. No ornamental chrome.
2. **Warm restraint** — natural tones, generous whitespace, nothing screams.
3. **Glanceable hierarchy** — the most important info (status, last value) reads in under 1 second.
4. **Wet-hands friendly** — large touch targets, haptic confirmation, minimal precision tapping.
5. **Earned complexity** — simple surface, depth on demand (tap for trends, scroll for nutrients).
