Reef Monitor — Track your reef aquarium water parameters

Monitor your nano reef parameters with precision and peace of mind. Built for dedicated reef keepers, Reef Monitor lets you log readings, visualize trends, and catch parameter drift before it becomes a problem.

QUICK LOGGING
• Large touch targets designed for wet hands
• +/- steppers tuned per parameter (0.1 dKH, 5 ppm Ca...)
• Haptic feedback on every save
• Built-in Salifert test timers (30 sec, 3 min)

8 ESSENTIAL PARAMETERS

• Automatic NO3:PO4 ratio and Ca/Alk/Mg ionic balance detection

DOSING LOG
