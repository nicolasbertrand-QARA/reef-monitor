Reef Monitor — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Summary

Reef Monitor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your data stays on your device.

Data Storage

All water parameter readings, dosing logs, and settings are stored locally on your device using SQLite. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

No Account Required

Reef Monitor does not require you to create an account or sign in. There is no user authentication.

No Analytics or Tracking

Reef Monitor does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. No usage data is collected.

No Network Access

Reef Monitor does not make any network requests. The app functions entirely offline.

Data Export

You can export your data as a CSV file using the built-in export feature. This file is shared via your device's share sheet and is never uploaded automatically.

Data Deletion

Deleting the app from your device removes all stored data permanently.
